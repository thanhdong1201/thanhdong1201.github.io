Privacy Policy for Italian Brainrot: IQ Mystery

Effective Date: May 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how BanhmiStudio, an individual developer ("I" or "Service Provider"), collects, uses, and protects information in the **Italian Brainrot: IQ Mystery** mobile application ("App"). The App is a fun, ad-supported quiz game designed for children aged 6-12, provided "AS IS."

Information I Collect

I limit data collection to keep the App safe for kids. The App may collect:

  • Device details (e.g., operating system).
  • Usage data (e.g., time spent playing, pages visited).

I do not collect:

  • Personal details (e.g., name, address).
  • Precise location data.

How I Use Information

I use this data to:

  • Comply with laws and ensure safety.

Third-Party Services

The App uses trusted services with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (kid-safe ads)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (anonymous data)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (error reports)
  • Unity (game engine)

I only share anonymous, grouped data to improve the App.

Children’s Privacy

The App complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) for kids aged 6-12. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such data is found, I delete it immediately.

Parents can email binshincute.2001@gmail.com to:

  • Review or delete data.
  • Ask about privacy practices.

Advertisements

The App shows kid-friendly ads to stay free. All ads are safe and follow Google Play’s family policies. Ads do not track personal information.

Data Retention

I keep data only as needed to run or improve the App. If you uninstall the App, data collection stops, and stored data is deleted soon after.

Opt-Out

Uninstall the App via your device’s standard process to stop data collection.

Security
